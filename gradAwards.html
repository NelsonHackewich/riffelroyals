Graduation Awards 2016

Graduation Award Applications due May 15th to Student Services

If you are interested in being considered for any of the Grade 12 awards please carefully read the qualifications needed for each award. Complete the application for each specific award. Include any pertinent information that may benefit your application in the comments section. Applications must be typed. Follow the appropriate format for each award. Each award you want to be considered for must have its own application form. Applications that do not follow these instructions will not be considered. Your application for these awards is considered your consent for distribution of résumé to Riffel Staff.

Awards

The Catholic Leadership Award

This Board-sponsored award is given to the student who has demonstrated Catholic leadership at both the school and parish level. This student has demonstrated care for others through his or her actions, attitudes and commitments and has a positive attitude toward students, staff, and the school so that she/he has won the respect of students and staff.

The Joseph McIntyre Award of Merit

This Board-sponsored award is given to the student who has made noteworthy academic progress corresponding with ability, but who is not necessarily on the honour roll and who has contributed strongly to school life through active participation in school activities.

Indigenous Student Achievement Award

This award, sponsored by the Regina Roman Catholic School Board, is presented:

  • to a graduating student of Aboriginal Ancestry who has academic standing of 65% or higher;
  • who is involved in extracurricular and/or community Aboriginal activities;
  • who has worked for or plans to work for the betterment of Aboriginal people;
  • and who plans to continue his/her education at a post-secondary institution

Aboriginal Student Leadership Award

This award is presented to a student of Aboriginal Ancestry, who is strong representative and role model for Aboriginal Students. He/She will have good academic standing and will be actively involved in community activities. Please include a resume of activities, work experience and post-secondary plans..

Knights of Columbus Council #1247 Achievement Award

This award is given to a student who is diligent in academic studies and who contributes to school and parish activities.

Sisters of Our Lady of the Missions Scholarship

This Catholic School Board-sponsored award is given to a female Grade 12 student who has an average above 75% in 20 and 30 classes, combined with community, extracurricular, and parish involvement and who plans on attending any post-secondary program. Two letters of recommendation are required - one from the priest/parish worker and one from a person not related to the family but have known the applicant for at least two years.

Holy Family Knights of Columbus Christian Attitude Award

This award is given to someone who lives out a Christian attitude and has a sincere concern for others.

Noble, Johnson, and Associates Student Scholarship

This scholarship is given to someone who completed Law 30 class in either their grade 11 or 12 year and must be entering a post-secondary program in the fall.
